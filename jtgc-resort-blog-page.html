JTGC Resort: Where Tranquility Meets Recreation

Unwinding Amidst Nature, Aug 12, 2020

Beyond the club's bustling activity lies the JTGC Resort—a sanctuary where nature and leisure intertwine seamlessly. Picture waking up to birdsong, sipping chai on your private veranda, and feeling the crisp mountain air. Here's what awaits you:

Cottages and Gardens Our cozy cottages blend rustic charm with modern amenities. Each cottage has a story—the "Sunflower Cottage" named after the sunflowers that bloom nearby, and the "Whispering Pines" cottage tucked away in the pine grove. Wander through landscaped gardens, where fragrant roses and vibrant marigolds greet you at every turn.

Adventure Awaits For the adventurous souls, JTGC offers guided treks to nearby waterfalls and viewpoints. The forest trails lead to hidden caves, where folklore whispers of ancient sages seeking enlightenment. As the sun sets, gather around the bonfire, sharing tales of your day's escapades.
